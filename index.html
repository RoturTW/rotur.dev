About Rotur

Rotur is a powerful WebSocket-based networking protocol and account system, specifically designed to enhance the connectivity of Web-Based operating systems. Our platform provides a seamless, unified experience across various applications with a focus on interconnectivity, social features and ease of use.

Features

Core Platform

Unified Account System

One account works everywhere. Sign in once and access any game or operating system that integrates with Rotur, creating a seamless cross-platform experience.

Advanced Networking

Built on an enhanced version of cloudlink, Rotur's networking layer transmits rich user data and context with each packet, enabling more sophisticated application interactions.

Universal Avatars

Access user profile images through our simple avatars.rotur.dev service. Just append any username to instantly retrieve their 256×256 JPEG profile picture for seamless UI integration.

Communication

Social Connections

Connect with friends across the Rotur ecosystem. Send and manage friend requests, see who's online, and interact with your network across all integrated platforms.

Rmail Messaging

Send and receive messages to any Rotur user through our built-in mail system. Communications persist across sessions and platforms for true connectivity.

Voice Communication

Make real-time voice calls between Rotur users on any platform. Developed in collaboration with CL5 and MikeDev, this feature enables seamless voice interaction.

Claw Social Platform

A Twitter-like social media platform built on Rotur. Claw features a simple, algorithm-free single feed that's perfect for adding social functionality to your Rotur client operating systems.

Storage & Synchronization

Cloud Storage

Store key-value pairs securely in the cloud. Perfect for game saves, user preferences, documents, and any data you want accessible across multiple devices and platforms.

Real-time Data Sync

Synchronize variables between peers with minimal latency. Build collaborative applications and multiplayer experiences with our advanced variable sync system.

Economy & Rewards

Rotur Economy

Our integrated currency system awards 1 credit daily to active users. Transfer credits between accounts and participate in the Rotur marketplace with this universal currency.

Digital Marketplace

Buy, sell, and trade digital items using Rotur Credits. The marketplace enables content creators to distribute assets and users to enhance their experience.

Achievement System

Collect and display badges on your profile to showcase your accomplishments across the Rotur ecosystem.
